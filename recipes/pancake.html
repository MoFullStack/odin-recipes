Pancake

Description

A pancake is a flat cake, often thin and round, prepared from a starch-based batter that may contain eggs, milk and butter, and then cooked on a hot surface such as a griddle or frying pan. It is a type of batter bread. Archaeological evidence suggests that pancakes were probably eaten in prehistoric societies.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Large eggs
  • Milk
  • Sunflower/Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients into a bowl or large jug, then whisk to smooth batter.
  2. Set aside for 30 minutes if you have time, otherwise start cooking.
  3. Set a medium frying pan or crepe pan over a medium heat and carefully wipe it with some oiled kitchen paper.
  4. When hot, cook the pancakes for 1 min on each side until goden, using around half a ladleful of batter per pancake.
  5. Cover with foil and bake
